LES FRAGMENTS D'UN DISCOURS AMOUREUX

New edition of Les Fragments d’un discours amoureux by Roland Barthes, where the layout of texts becomes a means of semantic interpretation, exploiting and repurposing all of InDesign’s tools to create unexpected compositions. This edition is the result of a collective effort, yielding a richly meaningful outcome.
