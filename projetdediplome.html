DEAFLYMPICS

For my diploma project, I designed the campaign for the 2026 Paris Deaflympics. The goal was to come up with a low-budget communication strategy that would give the event a spectacular dimension while highlighting its unifying power. Through a participatory campaign, the project emphasizes that the Deaflympics are not just a sporting competition, but also a time for sharing and meeting others, an event that creates a bond with the public.
This campaign is conducted in a fictional setting.
