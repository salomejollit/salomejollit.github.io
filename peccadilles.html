PECCADILLES
[packaging] [2025]

Peccadilles is reinventing snacking by making insect-based foods more accessible. To embody this mainstream approach, I designed striking packaging, repurposing the famous “Keep calm and eat Peccadilles” slogan to capture attention and normalize entomophagy. The symbols highlight the flavors and presence of insects through clear and playful iconography, transforming curiosity into a culinary and gourmet experience.
