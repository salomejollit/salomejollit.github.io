THE GLASS HALF FULL

Design of the graphic line for a newspaper that communicates good news. I focused on spontaneity through cut-out paper, which evokes the sincerity that can be found in reading good news. I designed a cut-out paper font that can be found in newspapers in order to create a strong identity for the magazine.


Animation of the newspaper's identity in After Effects to create an advertisement that will be broadcast in the subway.
