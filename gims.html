GIMS MERCHANDISING X OFFSTAGE
[merchandising] [2024]

Creation of a merchandising collection for summer 2024, aligned with Gims’ visual universe and current trends. The result is modern, impactful merch that resonates with a cross-generational audience.

This collaboration is a fictional collection between the artist Gims and the Offstage website.
