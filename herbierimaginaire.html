IMAGINARY HERBARIUM
[print/edition] [2024]

Creation of a risograph booklet. According to research, global warming will cause plants to grow larger and more voluminous. This led me to think of flowers taking revenge on humans to make them aware of the damage they have caused to plants through pollution. This booklet is a source of information to show future populations why flowers have undergone this transformation.
