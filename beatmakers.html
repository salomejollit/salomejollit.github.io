BEATMAKERS
[edition] [2023]

Design of an editorial collection concept consisting of showcasing the work of BeatMakers through Arte radio podcasts. Their signature instrument is embedded in their initials on the front and back covers, where soundtracks are featured, reflecting the rhythm and sequencing of sound.

CHOCOLAT
[packaging] [2022]

Creation of an identity for La Maison du Chocolat. The aim was to take consumers on a journey with a collection of packaging reflecting the cultural characteristics of a country. The principle of collage was used in this project, inspired by the aesthetic language of Mathilde AUBIER. I took the elements that make up the country's identity to create a pattern and highlight it on the front of the packaging.
